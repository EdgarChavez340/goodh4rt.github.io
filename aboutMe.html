About Me

My Inspiration:

Goodh4rt’s passion for music kicked off over eight years ago, when he joined a Young Musician Club in middle school.
He was hooked on the craft almost instantaneously and has remained dedicated to it ever since. Music has always been present in my life and has helped me through very tough situations” says Goodh4rt.

“It has helped me grow into the person I am today.” Throughout his artistic development, he has been inspired by various artists who have helped- and continued to help- shape his voice. These include the likes of XXXTentacion, Phora, Juice World, and Lil Peep. With his unique voice and dedication to thematic honesty, Goodh4rt is a clear rising star in the world of hip hop.
