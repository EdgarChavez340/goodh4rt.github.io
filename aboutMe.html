About Me

Goodh4rt’s passion for music kicked off over eight years ago, when he joined a Young Musician Club in middle school. He was hooked on the craft almost instantaneously and has remained dedicated to it ever since.

“Music has always been present in my life and has helped me through very tough situations,” says Goodh4rt, “It has helped me grow into the person I am today.”
